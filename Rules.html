Rules


These rules apply to our discord and everything related to this clan. Joking outside the clan is allowed

1. Be respectful of everyone and keep things cordial. Personal abuse, harassment, threats, hateful speech or language, discriminatory or defamatory remarks of any nature will NOT be tolerated.

4. Be mindful of and use the appropriate channels.

If you do not follow these rules then you might be kicked from our clan
